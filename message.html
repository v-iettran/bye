My Last Words

These words are for the friends I made in the last moments of college. I don't really know how to say thank you properly or how to act right, maybe because I've been distant from people for quite a while. My experience at DKU wasn't the best. I missed many opportunities, broke several relationships, and often felt lost. I guess that's just how life works sometimes.

Because of that, I became hesitant to open up to new people. I hated exposing myself and showing my vulnerability. When I started this position, I wanted nothing more than just a job. But what I received instead was a group of beautiful people who ended up fulfilling my college experience.
